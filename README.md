# 📊 Sales Performance Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Sales Performance Dashboard built in Power BI using the Sample Superstore dataset. The dashboard helps analyze sales, profit, customer segments, product performance, and regional trends through interactive visualizations and KPIs.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

The objective of this dashboard is to help business stakeholders:

- Monitor overall sales performance
- Analyze profit trends
- Identify top-performing products
- Compare regional sales
- Understand customer segment contribution
- Make data-driven business decisions

---

## 🛠 Tools Used

- Power BI Desktop
- Power Query
- DAX
- Microsoft Excel / CSV

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

### Overview Page

- Total Sales KPI
- Total Profit KPI
- Total Orders KPI
- Total Customers KPI
- Monthly Sales Trend
- Sales by Category
- Sales by Customer Segment
- Interactive Category Slicer

---

### Regional Analysis

- Sales Distribution Map
- Top States by Sales
- Top 10 Products
- Profit vs Discount Analysis
- Interactive Category Slicer

## 💡 Key Insights

- Technology generates the highest sales.
- Consumer segment contributes the largest share of sales.
- California is the highest-performing state.
- Higher discounts often reduce profit.
- Sales fluctuate across months with noticeable seasonal peaks.
